The department of Computer Technology was established in 1993. The head of department is Mr. R. Y.Gungandi. The total intake of students for the department is 90. Vision: To nurture proficient computer technocrats with ethical values to contribute to thedevelopment of society.

Mission:

  • M1: To impart knowledge and skills of computer engineering by effective implementation of teaching and learning methodologies.
  • M2: To inculcate ethics and professional skills in the students.
  • M3: To develop students with attitude of providing sustainable solutions to real world problems.
